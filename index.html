About Me

From the title my name is Allan and I was raised in Australia. I graduated from UNSW with a Bachelors in Commerce (Majors in Finance and Accounting) in 2016. Ever since then I have been working in various companies in the Accounting department.

When I joined my previous company as an junior accountant, I realised that there were so many changes I could make to their Excel sheets which were outdated and everyone had to manually enter in everything. I spent a few lunchbreaks fixing up all that which could be automated through VBA and generic Excel code. It was when I realised that figuring out how to code and debug things kept me my sanity and after which I decided to look towards Computer Science or Software/Web development as a future career.

I currently still work full time as an Accountant however I'm hoping that after this bootcamp I can start building my portfolio through freelancing or through creating my own projects. After I have a solid portfolio, I'll look into trying to obtain internships or seeing if I can get a full time job within a coding related field.
